About

I’m a Software Engineer with a strong focus on Deep Learning. I completed my Master’s in Computer Science, Intelligent Systems at Technical University Berlin, while working as a research assistant at Fraunhofer HHI. In my thesis, I explored a novel method to achieve general prediction stability of convolutional neural networks across various input distortions. Previously, I worked on relation extraction from open social media data at DFKI. During my studies, I also worked at IBM and as a freelancer for different clients, where I developed, migrated, and maintained various machine learning compontents. I’m interested in applying machine learning to solve meaningful problems.
